Tapenade (Olive Spread)

This is a really simple dish to prepare and it's always a bit hit at parties. My father recommends!

"Make this the night before so that the flavors have time to blend. Just bring it up to room temperature before you serve it. In winder, try serving it warm."

Ingredients

  • 8oz. sundried tomatoes
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2/3 kalamata olives
  • 1 capers

Instructions

Combine tomatoes and garlic in a food processor. Blend until as smooth as possible.

Add capers and olives. Pulse a few times until they are incorporated and retain texture.

Serve on thin toast rounds with goat cheese and fresh basil (optional).
